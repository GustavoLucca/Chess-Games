A chess game archive

Do you want to learn how to play attacking, agressive chess? You're in the right place!

Here, we offer a chess library of games played by some of the most prominent attacking masterminds of chess: Garry Kasparov, Marc Esserman, and Hikaru Nakamura. All games showcased in this website include thorough, meticulous analysis made by yours truly; the objective is for my notes and my game curation to help you understand attacking, sacrificial, and agressive chess better. On top of this, all of our pages include historical context and also other pieces of media related to the game or the players. I hope you enjoy!



About me

Hi! My name is Gustavo Lucca, and I play chess :)

I started playing chess when I was little, but I didn't really get into it until sophmore year of high school, when I started my school's chess club. That's when I started playing competitively at tournaments and teaching chess to others, two very different facets of the game that have shown me the dichotomy of chess. Ever since that start, I haven't stopped playing chess, with some of my greatest achievements so far including becoming the under-18 champion at a national level and qualifying to represent Puerto Rico at the 2022 Panamerican Chess Championship. With my playing style always being agressive and attacking, I decided to create this website to guide other players who desire to play wild, sacrificial chess. I hope you enjoy these games.
